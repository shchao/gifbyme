Free Animated GIF maker


GIF maker allows you to instantly create your animated GIFs by combining separated image files as frames.

Produced GIFs are of high quality and free of watermarks or attribution, making this tool ideal for developers and content creators.


To make a GIF, you can upload a sequence of JPG, PNG, BMP, TIFF, HEIC, AVIF, and other types of images, and even mix different formats and sizes - they will be converted automatically.


How to make a GIF?

Press the "upload image" area and select the images you want to use as frames.


You can press and hold the ctrl/command key to select multiple files.


When the images are uploaded, you can adjust animation speed and frame order before making the GIF.


Tips for GIF creation


You can control the speed for the whole GIF by setting a "Delay time" at the bottom.


Delay is in 1/1000 of a second between frames - a larger value causes the animation to appear slower.


It's not advisable to set delay time lower than 10 because some browsers will ignore such values and default to slower animation speed.


By default, all frames will be sorted in uploading order, you can drag and drop them inside the GIF animator area to rearrange.


If you upload images of different sizes, two options will appear: automatically resizing and cropping all images to match the smallest dimensions, or choosing the output dimensions and fitting them with the original aspect ratio in the specified dimension.
