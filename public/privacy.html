Privacy policy and use of cookies

This site uses cookies to check how many people visit (without knowing who you are), remember what you like, and show ads.


We don't keep personal info on our servers. If you think we have something of yours and want it gone, tell us


Files you upload stay on our servers for about 1 hour after you last use our tools. After that, we delete them safely. We don't show the names of your files to everyone.
